About Us

Capricorn Associates & Gemini Impex, run by Rtn. R.Shankar is a textile engineer by profession with a post graduation in Public Administration. After completing his college in 1984 he joined as a Service Engineer for world reputed textile machinery giant M/s W. Schlafhorst of Germany & M/s Vouk of Italy. In 1994, Capricorn Associates was launched to import and sell textile machinery.

  • Expertise in handling a diverse range of operations entailing Textile, Fabric operations, training and budgeting.
  • Proven track record of developing procedures & service standards.
  • Sold more than 1000 Looms & 200 Autoconers.
  • Operating based out of Africa, South America, Europe and Central Asia
  • Providing end to end services from machinery loading, production and post production operations
